"AI safety" is such a fascinating concept because of the massive disconnect between what's talked about publicly ("protecting mankind") and what the bulk of the effort and money is poured into ("don't let it praise Hitler and generate nudes because we'll get bad PR").

One interesting tangent here is that earlier internet tech wasn't under this kind of PR scrutiny to only show you sanitized views. The web didn't care if you wanted to read about Jewish space vaccines.
